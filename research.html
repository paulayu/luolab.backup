Current Research Projects

Our lab dedicated to bridging the traditional fields of wildlife ecology and biogeography to the cutting-edge advances in molecular genetics, genomics and bioinformatics.  The broad research theme is the genomic basis of diversity, evolution, and adaptation in mammalian species.  Specifically, our primary research covers several areas in both domestic and free-ranging mammalian species:

 

  1. Functional genomics of free-ranging wildlife
  1. Genomic studies of the domestic cat (Felis catus)
  1. Conservation genomics of endangered wildlife species
  1. Field ecology and landscape conservation of the tiger
